Attend a World Cup Final

The pinnacle of global sports energy. Billions watch — but being there in the stadium, feeling the roar, the suspense, the unity of nations… nothing compares.


  1. How I will obtain this goal
    • Research and select the World Cup event you want to attend.
    • Plan your budget, including travel, accommodation, and ticket costs.
    • Apply for tickets through official channels as soon as they become available.
    • Arrange travel and accommodation well in advance to secure the best options.
    • Familiarize yourself with the host country's culture and logistics for a smooth experience.

Extended Sailboat Trip

Open ocean, complete freedom, and a deeper connection with nature. A long sailboat journey means learning self-reliance, mastering the winds, and embracing life at its rawest.


  1. How I will obtain this goal
    • Obtain necessary sailing certifications and experience.
    • Plan your route, including stops and provisions needed.
    • Prepare your sailboat for long-term travel, ensuring all systems are functional.
    • Gather essential supplies, including food, water, and safety equipment.
    • Inform friends or family about your itinerary for safety purposes.

Kayak the Cape of Africa

A wild expedition at the meeting point of oceans — unpredictable, beautiful, and powerful. Kayaking the Cape is both a daring adventure and a spiritual journey.


  1. How I will obtain this goal
    • Research the best time of year for kayaking the Cape of Africa.
    • Acquire a suitable kayak and necessary gear for long-distance paddling.
    • Train physically to build endurance and strength for the journey.
    • Plan your route, including potential stopover points for rest and resupply.
    • Ensure you have safety measures in place, including communication devices and emergency plans.

Summit Mt. Rainier

A massive, iconic glacier-covered volcano. Summiting Mt. Rainier means endurance, skill, teamwork, and standing atop one of Americas most legendary peaks.


  1. How I will obtain this goal
    • Gain experience in mountaineering and glacier travel.
    • Join a guided expedition or hire a certified guide for the climb.
    • Train physically to build strength, endurance, and acclimatization to altitude.
    • Prepare necessary gear, including clothing, climbing equipment, and safety gear.
    • Plan your ascent carefully, considering weather conditions and route options.
